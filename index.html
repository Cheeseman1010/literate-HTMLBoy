What's the Point of this Site?

While messing around trying to come up with an idea for a website for this project, I had the questionably good idea to make a simple platforming game in a browser. A couple Google searches later, I had the idea to place it into a GameBoy, and now we're here.

In order to "win" the game, just advance forward to the "flagpole" at the end of the course.


Controls:

The game moves frame by frame, so it won't advance until you press a button. So to fall down after jumping, just push another button that won't do anything. (Up, Down, B, A)

  • A - To Jump
  • Left - Move Left
  • Right - Move Right
  • Down - Quick fall to Ground (while airborne)
  • Power - Turns on and off console
  • Reset - Sets player to spawn while game is running
